Section 5 of 6 — Willingness to Pay
How much would you pay for this service? *
This question uses a special method to get your honest maximum. Please read the instructions carefully before entering your amount.
WTP_BDM
Maximum Monthly Payment


Enter the maximum amount you would be willing to pay per month for this health service (between ₹0 and ₹2,000).

After you submit, our system will randomly generate a price. If your amount is equal to or higher than the random price, you will purchase the service at the random price. If your amount is lower, you will not be charged anything.

Because of this rule, it is always in your best interest to enter your true maximum. Entering a higher number than your true maximum could mean you pay more than you want. Entering a lower number could mean you miss out.
